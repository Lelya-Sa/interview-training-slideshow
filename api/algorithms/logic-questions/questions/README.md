## How to Use

1. **Start with Brain Teasers**: These are perfect for junior developers
2. **Think First**: Try to solve it yourself before reading the solution
3. **Understand the Approach**: Learn the reasoning process, not just the answer
4. **Practice Variations**: Work through follow-up questions
5. **Explain Out Loud**: Practice articulating your thought process

## Tips for Interview Success

- **Think Out Loud**: Explain your reasoning step by step
- **Ask Questions**: Clarify constraints and assumptions
- **Start Simple**: Begin with basic approach, then refine
- **Show Your Work**: Write down your thinking process
- **Check Your Answer**: Verify the solution makes sense

## Question Format

Each question file contains:
- **Problem**: Clear problem statement
- **Approach**: High-level strategy and reasoning
- **Solution**: Step-by-step explanation
- **Follow-up**: Related questions to explore deeper
